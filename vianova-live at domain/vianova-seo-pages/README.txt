ViaNova SEO Pages - Netlify Upload Instructions

1. Unzip this folder.
2. Copy all files into your Netlify project folder.
3. If you already have an index.html, do not overwrite it unless you want to.
4. Upload/deploy to Netlify.
5. Check these URLs after deployment:
   /career-assessment-for-students.html
   /stream-selection-after-10th.html
   /career-counselling-for-schools.html
   /aptitude-test-for-students.html
   /career-roadmap-for-students.html
6. Submit /sitemap.xml in Google Search Console.
7. Replace https://www.vianova.co.in in files if your final domain is different.
