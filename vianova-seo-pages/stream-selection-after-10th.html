Why stream selection goes wrong

Many students pick Science, Commerce or Humanities because of peer pressure, family expectations or marks. This often leads to stress, low motivation and career confusion later.

How ViaNova helps

ViaNova analyses traits, interests, aptitude and learning preferences to help students understand which stream may be a better fit.

Science, Commerce, Humanities or Liberal Arts?

Frequently asked questions

Which stream is best after 10th?

There is no single best stream. The right stream depends on a student's interests, aptitude, personality and career goals.

Can Commerce be chosen without Maths?

Yes, but the suitability depends on future career plans and college requirements.

Is Humanities good for high-paying careers?

Yes. Humanities can lead to careers in law, public policy, design, psychology, media, civil services and more.
