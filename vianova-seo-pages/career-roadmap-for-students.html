Why students need a roadmap

Students often know career names but not the path required to reach them. A roadmap connects stream, subjects, entrance exams, degrees and job options.

What a good roadmap includes

A strong roadmap includes suitable streams, subject combinations, relevant entrance exams, degree or diploma options and alternate career paths.

Avoiding wrong turns

Without guidance, students may choose a stream without understanding eligibility rules or future limitations. A roadmap reduces such mistakes.

Frequently asked questions

When should a student create a career roadmap?

Ideally from Class 8 or 9, and definitely before choosing a stream after Class 10.

Can a roadmap change later?

Yes. A roadmap should evolve as interests, skills and goals become clearer.

Does ViaNova suggest entrance exams?

Yes. Career planning can include relevant entrance exams and study paths.
