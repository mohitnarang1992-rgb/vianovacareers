What is aptitude?

Aptitude is a student's natural ability to learn, reason, solve problems and understand patterns. It is different from school marks.

What the test checks

The ViaNova aptitude test can include numerical reasoning, verbal reasoning, logical reasoning, spatial ability and mechanical thinking indicators.

Why it matters

A student may score well in a subject but still feel mismatched with a stream. Aptitude insights help make choices with more confidence.

How results should be used

Aptitude should not be used alone. It works best when combined with interest, personality and work-value insights.

Frequently asked questions

Is an aptitude test enough for career selection?

No. It should be combined with interests, personality and career goals.

Can Class 8 students take it?

Yes. A simplified version can help younger students explore strengths early.

Does low aptitude mean a student cannot choose a career?

No. It simply indicates areas that may need more support or practice.
